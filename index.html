Getting There

Tirana International Airport (TIA) is the main airport serving the city.

From the airport, the city center can be reached in about 25–30 minutes by taxi or airport shuttle.

🏨

Hotels

For guests traveling from abroad, there are many beautiful hotels in Tirana. We recommend staying near the city center for easy access to the wedding venue and local attractions.

  • Maritim Hotel Plaza Tirana
  • Rogner Hotel Tirana
  • Lot Boutique Hotel

Explore Tirana

Tirana is a vibrant city full of culture, great food and beautiful scenery.

If you have time during your stay, we highly recommend exploring the city, enjoying Albanian cuisine and discovering the unique charm of the area.
